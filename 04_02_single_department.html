Cardiology

Cardiology is a medical specialty and a branch of internal medicine concerned with disorders of the heart. It deals with the diagnosis and treatment of such conditions as congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, electrophysiology, heart failure and valvular heart disease.

  • Evaluating patients' cardiovascular health by performing exams or tests

  • Analyzing test results and diagnosing a patient's condition

  • Developing a treatment plan and explaining it to patients and their families

  • Documenting a patient's treatment plan to update and maintain records

  • Educating patients about potential cardiovascular problems and recommending prevention techniques, such as dietary changes

  • Performing emergency procedures for patients, such as those experiencing a heart attack

  • Researching cardiovascular conditions to stay updated on advancements in their field
